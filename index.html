Rattlesnake Ledge

  • Location: Near Snoqualmie, WA
  • 605 ft. tall and 138 ft. wide
  • A moderately difficult trail that offers stunning views of Rattlesnake Lake and surrounding mountains, featuring a well-marked forest path perfect for families and shorter treks.

Beneath the Streets Underground Tour

  • Location: Centered in the Pioneer Square Seattle neighborhood
  • Tour Highlights: Guided tours with historic site visits
  • Size: The Underground Tour tunnels span about 20 blocks total
  • Historical context: From the Great Seattle Fire of 1889, which prompted the city to raise its streets to address flooding issues, leaving the original storefronts underground, now featured in Seattle Underground tours.

For more information, visit here.

Fremont Troll

  • Location: Under the Aurora Bridge, Fremont neighborhood in Seattle, WA
  • A large 18 ft highconcrete sculpture of a troll grasping a Volkswagen Beetle
  • Artist: Created by a team of artists with sculptor Steven Badanes in 1990
